SPECIALTY

Advanced Disinfection for Maximum Protection

Electrostatic disinfection is one of the most effective ways to eliminate germs, bacteria, and airborne contaminants from surfaces. This innovative method charges disinfectant particles, allowing them to wrap around and fully coat surfaces, ensuring 360-degree coverage.

  • Ideal for high-traffic spaces such as medical offices, schools, and commercial buildings
  • Eliminates 99.9% of bacteria and viruses, including flu and COVID-19
  • Safe for electronics, furniture, and hard-to-reach areas

    • With MidSouth Cleaning's electrostatic spraying service, you can ensure that your facility remains disinfected, safe, and compliant with health standards.
